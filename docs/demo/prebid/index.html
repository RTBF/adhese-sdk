Adhese Prebid Demo

This page demonstrates the Adhese and Improve Digital Prebid adapters active on the same page. The Adhese adpater will yield between all it's configured server-side resources, in this demo direct campaigns and Rubicon's XAPI.

At the same time, before the page is even fully loaded, Improve Digital is called via it's own adapter. They respond with a bid, the net ecpm of both bids is compared and the heighest is shown on the page.
